What is FarmoTech?

FarmoTech is a revolutionary platform designed to bring income stability to farmers and supply assurance to buyers. By digitizing the contract farming process, we eliminate middlemen, ensuring farmers get fair prices locked before the season starts, while buyers get quality-verified produce delivered on time.

Automated Payment & Settlement

Instant fund release upon delivery confirmation.
